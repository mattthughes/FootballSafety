About us

We as a company are focused on improving safety at football, we will do this by highlighting football disasters showcasing the causes and who was to blame. We are commited to making sure stories such as Hillsborough are not forgotten, that the victims and their familys are remembered for all the right reasons. We will make football a safer place for all of our friends, family and most importantly our children.
